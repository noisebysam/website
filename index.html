About

Sam is a seasoned mixing and mastering engineer based in Milton Keynes who has been shaping sound for over a decade. Known for clarity, punch and musicality, Sam’s passion lies in helping artists achieve their sonic vision and delivering mixes and masters that translate seamlessly across all playback systems.

Whether you’re an independent artist or a major label signee, Sam provides personalised attention and a commitment to excellence on every project. Bringing a unique blend of warmth, character and precision to your music, Sam can take your sound to the next level and make your music stand out.
